Privacy Policy - FixItNow

Last Updated: March 2026

FixItNow is a service booking platform that connects customers with technicians (electricians, plumbers, etc.). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • User Role (Customer / Technician)
  • Profile Details

1.2 Location Data

  • Customers: Used to find nearby technicians
  • Technicians: Used for real-time tracking during service

1.3 Usage Data

  • Device information
  • App activity and interactions
  • Service booking history

1.4 Payment Data

All payments are processed securely via third-party gateways. We do NOT store your card or banking details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To connect customers with technicians
  • To enable booking, tracking, and communication
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To send notifications and updates

3. Data Security

  • Secure authentication systems (Firebase/Auth APIs)
  • Encrypted communication (HTTPS)
  • Access control based on user roles

4. User Rights

  • Update your personal data
  • Delete your account
  • Request data removal
  • Opt-out of notifications

5. Third-Party Services

  • Authentication services (Firebase or similar)
  • Maps & location services (Google Maps API)
  • Payment gateways

These third parties are responsible for handling your data securely under their policies.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected within the app.
